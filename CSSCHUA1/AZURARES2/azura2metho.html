Methodology

MATERIALS THAT WILL BE USED:
The following materials will be used in the experiment:
1. Lanzones peels
2. Blender
3. Distilled water
4. Spray bottle
5. Sliced bread
6. Clear containers
7. Gloves and mask


PROCEDURE
1.10 Lanzones peels will be dried under the sun for a day.
2. The dried peels will be blended into small pieces.
3. About 100 ml of distilled water will be added to the peels and left to soak for 48 hours.
4. The extract will then be filtered using a strainer.
5. The liquid extract will be transferred into a spray bottle.
6. The liquid extract will be sprayed about 10cm away from the bread
7. Bread slices will be divided into two groups: one will be sprayed with the extract, and the other will not be sprayed. This experimentation will consider the amount of sprayed product onto the bread.
8. All samples will be placed in containers at room temperature and observed for 7 days.

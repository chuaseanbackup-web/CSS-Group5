All About Lanzones

The Lanzones (Lansium domesticum) fruit is oval, ovoid-oblong, or nearly round, measuring 2.5-5 cm in diameter. This skin is characterized by its light greyish-yellow to pale brownish or pink color and a velvety texture. It can be leathery, thin, or thick, and is notable for containing milky latex (Orwa et al., 2009).

Research Objective

The objective of this study is to examine how plastic debris accumulates in the Marikina River during flood events using Large-Scale Particle Image Velocimetry (LSPIV). Specifically, the study aims to analyze the surface flow velocity of the river during flooding, identify areas where plastic debris is most likely to accumulate, and determine which flow characteristics influence this accumulation. study aims to provide useful insights that can help improve flood management and river cleanup efforts in Marikina City.

Statement of the Problem

This study aims to determine the nesting patterns of janitor fish (Pterygoplichthys spp.) using Morphological Operations along the Marikina River. Specifically, it seeks to answer the following questions:

• What is the estimated concentration of janitor fish nests along different sections of the Marikina River based on the digital image?

• Are janitor fish nests clustered in areas with stable current flow according to morphological analysis?

• How does river current intensity influence the nesting patterns of janitor fish as detected through morphological operations?
